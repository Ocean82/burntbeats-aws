## 🚨 **2. COPYRIGHT INFRINGEMENT NOTICES**

### **2.1 How to Submit a DMCA Notice**

To report copyright infringement, send a notice to our DMCA agent with the following information:

**Required Information:**
- **Physical or electronic signature** of copyright owner or authorized agent
- **Identification** of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
- **Identification** of the material claimed to be infringing (with specific URL)
- **Contact information** including name, address, phone, and email
- **Statement** that you have good faith belief the use is not authorized
- **Statement** that the information is accurate and you are authorized to act

## ⚡ **3. RESPONSE PROCEDURES**

### **3.1 Immediate Actions**
Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:
- **Acknowledge receipt** within 24 hours
- **Remove or disable access** to allegedly infringing material
- **Notify the user** who posted the material
- **Preserve evidence** for legal proceedings

### **3.2 User Notification**
We will notify the user who posted the material:
- **Identification** of the removed material
- **Copy** of the DMCA notice
- **Instructions** for filing a counter-notification
- **Warning** about repeat infringement

### **3.3 Repeat Infringer Policy**
Users who receive multiple DMCA notices:
- **First offense:** Warning and content removal
- **Second offense:** Temporary account suspension
- **Third offense:** Permanent account termination
- **Legal action** may be pursued

---

## 🔄 **4. COUNTER-NOTIFICATION PROCESS**

### **4.1 How to File a Counter-Notification**

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, you may file a counter-notification:

**Required Information:**
- **Physical or electronic signature**
- **Identification** of the removed material and its location
- **Statement** under penalty of perjury that you have good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
- **Name, address, phone** and consent to jurisdiction
- **Statement** that you consent to service of process

### **4.2 Counter-Notification Response**
Upon receiving a valid counter-notification:
- **Acknowledge receipt** within 24 hours
- **Forward to original complainant** within 10 days
- **Restore material** after 10 business days unless:
  - Original complainant files court action
  - We receive court order to maintain removal
